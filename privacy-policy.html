Privacy Policy

At CVenesca Group LLC, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide to us through our website.

1. Information We Collect

Through our contact form, we collect the following personal information, which you voluntarily provide:

  • Name: To identify and address you appropriately.
  • Email: To be able to respond to your inquiry.
  • Message: The content of your inquiry or comment.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Respond to your questions and inquiries.
  • Communicate with you about the services you have requested.
  • Improve our services and user experience.

Your data will not be shared, sold, or used for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. Social Media Links

Our website includes links to social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp. By clicking on these links, you leave our site and are subject to the privacy policies of those external platforms.

4. Data Storage and Security

For email processing, we use a secure third-party service called EmailJS. Your information is protected by SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption, which ensures that the communication between your browser and our website is secure.

We do not store your personal information on our own servers. The information is transmitted directly through our email service to allow us to respond to you.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. If you have any questions or wish to exercise these rights, please contact us via email.
